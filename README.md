# 🇪🇹 Exam-app: Grade 12 National Exam Simulation Tool

An intuitive, accessible, and inclusive online exam simulation platform specifically designed for Ethiopian Grade 12 students, bridging the digital divide between urban and rural education.

---

## 📌 The Problem Statement
Since 2016 E.C., the Ethiopian Grade 12 National Examination has transitioned to an online platform. While this is a massive step forward for technology, it has created a critical challenge: **students in rural areas and those with limited exposure to technology struggle to navigate the online exam environment.** This digital anxiety directly impacts their performance, not due to lack of knowledge, but due to lack of familiarity with digital testing interfaces.

## 💡 The Solution
**Exam-app** is built to solve this exact problem. It provides a realistic, 100% stress-free replica of the national exam environment. By practicing on this platform, students from any background can build confidence, master time management, and eliminate technology-induced anxiety before the actual exam day.

---

## ✨ Key Features (Current & Upcoming)

* ⏱️ **Real-time Countdown Timer:** Replicates the strict timing of the national exam and auto-submits when time runs out.
* 📱 **Low-Tech Friendly (UI/UX):** Extremely simple, clean, and distraction-free user interface designed for absolute beginners.
* 📶 **Offline-First Capability (In Progress):** Built to withstand rural internet fluctuations, saving progress locally and syncing when connected.
* 🌍 **Local Language Guidance (Planned):** Integration of local languages (Amharic, Afaan Oromoo, Tigrinya, etc.) for instructions to ensure no student is left behind.
* 📊 **Instant Analytics:** Provides immediate scores and detailed explanations for wrong answers to foster continuous learning.
